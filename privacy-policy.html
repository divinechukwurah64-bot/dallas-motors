Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Dallas Motors ("Dallas Motors," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our showroom, use this website, or contact us about a vehicle, and how we use, share, and protect that information.

Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly, and a smaller amount of information collected automatically when you use this site.

  • Contact and enquiry details — name, phone number, email address, and any message you submit through our contact form, including the vehicle you're asking about.
  • Test drive and financing requests — driver's license details and financing information you choose to provide in person or by phone, handled by our sales and finance team.
  • Trade-in information — details about a vehicle you're trading in, including make, model, mileage, and condition.
  • Website usage data — pages viewed, vehicles favorited during your session, and general device/browser information. Favorites on this site are stored only in your browser's session storage and are not sent to us or any third party.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and book test drives.
  • To prepare financing estimates and process purchases.
  • To follow up about a vehicle you've expressed interest in.
  • To improve our inventory, pricing, and website based on aggregate, de-identified trends.
  • To meet legal, tax, and regulatory obligations related to vehicle sales.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Financing and insurance partners you choose to work with, solely to process your application.
  • Service providers who help us operate (such as payment processors), under confidentiality obligations.
  • Government or regulatory bodies where required by law, such as vehicle registration authorities.

Data retention

We retain enquiry and purchase records for as long as needed to serve you, honor warranties, and meet our legal and tax obligations, after which records are securely deleted or anonymized.

Your choices

  • You can ask us what personal information we hold about you, request a copy, or ask us to correct or delete it, subject to legal retention requirements (for example, sale records).
  • You can opt out of marketing follow-ups at any time by replying "stop" to any message or emailing us using the address below.
  • You can clear your favorited vehicles at any time by clearing your browser's session storage, or simply by closing your browser tab.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information you share with us. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your information appropriately for its sensitivity.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults purchasing or enquiring about vehicles. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do, and material changes will be posted here.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent to hello@dallasmotors.example or by post to 1420 Constitution Avenue, Central Business District, Abuja, Nigeria.
