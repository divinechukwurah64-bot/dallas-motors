Our approach

One standard, applied to every vehicle we sell

Most dealerships specialize — one brand, one segment. We took the opposite bet: build a single rigorous inspection and pricing standard, then apply it across sedans, SUVs, trucks, electric, sports, and luxury. The result is a lot where a first-time sedan buyer and a repeat truck buyer get the same transparent process.

Every vehicle — new, certified pre-owned, or trade-in — clears the same 150-point inspection before it's priced and listed. We publish the market comparison alongside the sticker so you can see the math, not just trust it.
