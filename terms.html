Terms of Sale

Last updated: July 14, 2026

These terms cover the purchase of a vehicle from Dallas Motors, whether new, certified pre-owned, or as-is trade-in stock. By placing a deposit or signing a purchase agreement with us, you agree to the terms below alongside the specific purchase agreement you sign in person.

Pricing

Listed prices reflect the vehicle's condition following our 150-point certified inspection and are shown before applicable taxes, registration, and government fees, which are calculated at the time of sale. Prices are subject to change until a deposit is placed, at which point your price is locked for 14 days or until financing is finalized, whichever is later.

Deposits and reservations

  • A refundable deposit holds a vehicle for 14 days while financing, inspection, or paperwork is finalized.
  • Deposits are fully refundable if Dallas Motors is unable to deliver the vehicle as described, or if financing is declined through no fault of the buyer.
  • Deposits are non-refundable if the buyer withdraws after the vehicle has passed final delivery inspection and been accepted in writing.

Financing

Financing estimates shown on this website (including the payment calculator on each vehicle page) are illustrative only, based on the estimated APR shown, and are not a loan offer. Final rate, term, and monthly payment depend on credit approval through our financing partners and are confirmed in a separate financing agreement before purchase.

Trade-ins

Trade-in valuations are estimates based on the information and photos you provide, and are confirmed in person following a physical inspection. The confirmed trade-in value will be applied as a credit toward your purchase and detailed on your final purchase agreement.

Vehicle condition and inspection

Every vehicle we sell — new, certified pre-owned, or trade-in — passes our 150-point inspection before listing, and the inspection report is available on request prior to purchase. Certified pre-owned vehicles are sold with the manufacturer or Dallas-backed warranty stated on the listing. Trade-in and as-is stock, where explicitly marked as such, is sold without warranty beyond what's stated in writing at the time of sale.

Warranty

New and certified pre-owned vehicles include the warranty coverage stated on the vehicle's listing and purchase agreement at the time of sale. Extended service plans are available for purchase separately and are detailed in their own agreement.

Cancellation

Once a purchase agreement is signed and the vehicle has been accepted following final delivery inspection, the sale is final except where required otherwise by applicable consumer protection law. Before final acceptance, either party may cancel per the deposit terms above.

Delivery

Vehicles are made available for pickup at our showroom once financing, registration, and final inspection are complete. Home or office delivery can be arranged for an additional fee, quoted at the time of purchase.

Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws applicable in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja, Nigeria, without regard to conflict-of-law principles.

Contact us

Questions about a specific purchase agreement should go to your sales advisor directly. General questions about these terms can be sent to hello@dallasmotors.example.
